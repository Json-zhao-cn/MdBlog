# **Factory three core systems**
There are threr core basic system in the manufacture industry.They are ERP, MES and SCADA/PLC system.

---

# MES vs ERP vs SCADA (Simple but Industry-Accurate)

| System        | Focus                                    | Time Scale             | Users                             |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **ERP**       | Business planning, finance, supply chain | Weeks / days           | Management, office                |
| **MES**       | Execution & control of manufacturing     | Hours / minutes        | Engineers, supervisors, operators |
| **SCADA/PLC** | Machine-level monitoring & control       | Seconds / milliseconds | Technicians, automation engineers |
