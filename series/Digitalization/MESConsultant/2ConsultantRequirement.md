## MES Developer/Consultant Skill Requirements
---
### 1. Professional Levels

- **Primary**: 1–3 years of experience in MES maintenance, implementation or development
- **Intermediate**: 3–5 years of full-cycle project experience
- **Senior**: 5+ years of MES working experience
- **Expert / Consultant**: 5+ years of experience; able to design solutions and lead projects
---

### 2. Basic Requirements
- **Degree**: College diploma or above; bachelor’s degree preferred
- **Major**: Computer Science, Automation, Industrial Engineering, Mechatronics, Software Engineering, or related engineering fields;
  Cross-major candidates with relevant experience are acceptable

---

### 3. Core Competencies
#### 3.1 MES Core
- Solid understanding of MES system architecture
- Proficient in core MES modules:
  Production Order Management, Production Reporting, Material Traceability, Quality Management, OEE, Process Management
- Familiarity with mainstream MES platforms (preferred but not mandatory):
  - International: Camstar, Apriso
  - Domestic: Orbitmes, Digiwin, Sei

#### 3.2 Database & Development
- Proficient in relational databases (Oracle, SQL Server, MySQL)
  or NoSQL databases (Redis, MongoDB)
- Skilled in SQL: CRUD, stored procedures, index & performance optimization
- Proficient in at least one programming language: Java / C# / Python
- System integration: Experience in interfacing with ERP, WMS, PLM

---

### 4. Preferred Qualifications
- Familiar with factory equipment; knowledge of industrial protocols such as OPC UA, Modbus
- Experience in **3C, Lithium Battery, PCB, Automotive / Commercial Vehicles, Semiconductor, Photovoltaic** manufacturing
- Understanding of production line workflow & process (core advantage)

---

### 5. Advanced Preferred Qualifications
- Hands-on experience in **new factory MES deployment, legacy system replacement, on-site implementation & project delivery**
- Fluent in written and spoken English;
  Experience in smart factory / digital factory projects or overseas IT projects
